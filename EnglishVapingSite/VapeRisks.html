Risks

Even if propylene glycol is quite harmless on its own, the health risks seen from its trace metals
are concerning. Propylene glycol can cause:

Usage

Propylene glycol is used in vapes for its humectant property, as well as its sugary taste. It works as a cheap solvent, allowing flavoring additives to be mixed with ease.

The Inadvertent Outcome

The inadvertent outcome of propylene glycol is quite concerning. This is because when in the mixing process, propylene glycol can pull in trace metals like lead, chromium, and nickel into the juice which can be severely toxic and dangerous. This issue is known by the corporations, yet, they turn a blind eye to it, allowing for cheap manufacturing costs.
