Risks

People think that just because tobacco is a plant, and is one ingredient it won't to that much harm;
but because of all the underlying chemicals and compounds inside tobacco, tobacco can cause:

Usage

Tobacco is the main component of a cigarette. When the tip of the cigarette is lit it combusts the tobacco releasing nicotine which is related to the buzzing feeling you experience when smoking. The industry has taken on flavoring tobacco as well to help pull in a broader consumer base.

The Inadvertent Outcome

People didnt realize the severe health implications of cigarettes until quite recently. Cigarettes on their own have more than 7,000 harmful compounds just lying in the tobacco.  As flavorings were added, the industry expanded making tobacco products like dip, snuff, and other tobacco products streamline, burying the implications, causing people to focus on the leisure more than the risks.

Risks

Paper doesn't seem like that harmful of a item. Yet, cigarette rolling papers can cause:

Usage

Cigarette rolling paper is used to pack cigarettes into small cyliners in which people can then causally smoke. It also is comprised of a filter, which helps affirm some regard for the public's health by these corporations.

The Inadvertent Outcome

Even though cigarettes have filters, they are really lack luster in their efficiency. This is because you still inhale tons of toxic compounds that go through the filter. This helps secure a false sense of security toward these products. The rolling papers are also dangerous on their own as they are made impure which allows harmful compounds to stay in the paper, causing you to inhale tar and other damaging substances.
