Question:


Why is vaping dangerous for teens?

Answer:


When you are young, your brain is still developing. When you take nicotine at a young age it can impair your cognitative abilities like your attention span and problem solving skills as stated by the CDC. Nicotine also causes severe addiction at a young age as the young brain is sensitive to the addictive quality of nicotine.

Question:


What are disposable vapes?

Answer:


Disposable vapes are vapes which only can be used until their pod is empty and are intended to be disposed of afterwords as the name suggests. They are designed to be more sleek and casual than traditional vapes because they dont have to deal with a refillable design and can be enclosed and compact. They aren't compositionally different and contain the same ingredients as traditional vapes and have the same threat to your health.

Question:


Are vapes ever safe to use?

Answer:


No; vapes-even when used as a cessation-can cause some severe physical harm. This has been proven by the CDC, as well as other independant organizations. Vapes also contain nicotine, which can encourage the brain to become reliant on the compound, helping you become even more addicted to tobacco products when you are supposed to be moving away from this industry.

Question:


Is there danger to second-hand smoke from a vape?

Answer:


Yes; when someone who vapes exhales the smoke it does still contain some amounts of the compounds being combusted like nicotine. These can then be easily inhaled by the second individual causing the same health risks than the user.

Question:


Is there advantages to using vapes instead of cigarrettes?

Answer:


It's needed to be exclaimed that nor vapes or cigarettes are safe by any means. But, when comparing the long lasting change that could be made when using vapes instead of cigarettes, vapes do prevail. This is because vapes are seen to be less harmful for pregnant women as stated by the CDC, and work as a proper cigarette cessation when used responsibly.

Question:


What concerns people most regarding vaping?

Answer:


The main concern people have with vaping is the prevalent risk of death and cancers. The CDC also mentions the tallying of deaths for unknown reasons related to vaping, showing the sketchy nature this industry holds.
